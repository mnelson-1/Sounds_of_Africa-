Read Yemi Alade's Biography

Yemi Alade is a Nigerian Afropop singer and songwriter known for her hit singles like "Johnny" and her powerful stage presence. Dubbed the "Queen of Afropop," she has received multiple international awards and has toured extensively across Africa and the globe.
