The Cavemen's Biography

The Cavemen is a Lagos based 'Afrocentric influenced' band comprising of Kingsley and his brother Benjamin James. The Cavemen are known for their unique sound which is a fusion of highlife, jazz and soul music. The band has released several hit songs including 'ADAUGO', 'Anita' and 'Me You I'.
